Chien Lu

Chien.Lu@tuni.fi/ B2026/ 0503182588

I am a doctoral researcher at Tampere University under the supervision of Jaakko Peltonen and Timo Nummenmaa. I am a member of SMiLE research group and CoE GameCult. My research focuses on Bayesian approach and its applications to game culture studies. I have also cooperated with researchers from other disciplines such as business and management and software development.

Research

Bayesian methodology development

My research focuses on text mining related problems such as multiple sources, underlying temporal dynamics and collective analysis.

Collatorative Works

Currently, I mainly cooperate with researchers from CoE GameCult, we focus on issues such as gamification, player studies, and game developement studies.
